## Selection Guidance

- Use at least two entries for narrow premium correction.
- Use at least three entries for product screens or dashboard direction.
- Use at least four entries for broad design-system direction.
- Use category contrast when avoiding template-like convergence.
- Always map each influence to concrete token, component, state, layout, or
  forbidden-pattern consequences.

## Non-Authority Rule

truth, local contracts, generated theme CSS, browser proof, or product-specific
design decisions.
