This surface governs whether the runtime can:

- reject technically correct but compositionally weak premium surfaces

- preserve dominant panel and dominant CTA from approved references
- prevent `card-soup`, `cta-dilution`, `flat-hierarchy`, and
  `reference-as-moodboard`

- premium billing recovery / upgrade surface
