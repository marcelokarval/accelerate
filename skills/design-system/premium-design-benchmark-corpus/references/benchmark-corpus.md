design libraries. It is intentionally compact: Accelerate needs enforceable
benchmark judgment, not a moodboard dump.

## Selection Rules

- Pick by product problem, not personal taste.
- Use fewer benchmarks with stronger influence rather than many weak citations.
- Every selected benchmark must change a token, component, state, layout, or
  forbidden-pattern rule.
- If a benchmark only contributes a vibe, remove it.
